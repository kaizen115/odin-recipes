Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic and diverse meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce of your choosing
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • *Optional* Pepperoni's
  • *Optional* Vegetables

Steps:

  1. Roll out the dough into a circle
  2. Add the sauce
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Add your choice of toppings
  5. Cook in oven for 12-18 minutes at 375 degrees
