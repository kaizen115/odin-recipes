Steak

Description

Steak is a simple yet intricate dish that's perfect for many occasions.

Ingredients

  • A cut of steak
  • Grass fed butter
  • Seasoning
  • Herbs

Steps:

  1. Heat pan to medium-high heat
  2. Add the butter and herbs
  3. Season the steak
  4. Add the steak and sear 4-6 minutes each side
  5. Once you have a nice crust all over, take off heat
  6. Enjoy.
