Chili

Description

Chili is a hearty soup-like dish typically consisting of beans, vegetables, meat and seasoning.

It is great during winter months to stay warm, indoors or outdoors.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Ground Beef
  • Celery
  • Seasoning
  • *Optional* Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan
  2. Dump chili beans and celery into a seperate pot
  3. Simmer on medium heat and add the ground beef
  4. Add seasoning and stir while it slowcooks
  5. Enjoy.
